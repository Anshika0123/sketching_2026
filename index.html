Our Story

Passion Rendered in
Every Stroke

Creative Expression was born from a simple, unshakeable belief: that art in all its forms deserves a dedicated, beautiful home. We are a collective of artists — painters, sketchers, and digital creators — united by an obsession with craft and the human stories that art can tell.

Our journey began in a sunlit studio in 2012, where founder Anshika Jain began sharing her oil paintings online. What started as a personal archive quickly grew into a thriving community of artists and collectors drawn to authentic, original work.

Today, Creative Expression showcases three distinct disciplines — traditional oil painting, pencil and graphite sketching, and cutting-edge digital art — each given the same reverence and presentation quality. No medium is lesser; only intentions differ.

Our Values

What Drives Us

🎨

Authenticity

Every work is an authentic original — no reproductions, no mass prints. We celebrate the irreplaceable presence of hand-made and hand-rendered art that carries the artist's true spirit.

✏️

Mastery of Craft

From understanding pigment behaviour in oil to mastering digital brush dynamics, we believe in the long, patient study of one's medium. Skill and dedication elevate art above decoration.

🤝

Community

We nurture lasting relationships between creators and collectors — building a community grounded in mutual appreciation and the shared love of visual storytelling.

Meet the Artist

The Creative Mind
Behind It All

Anshika Jain

Founder & Lead Artist

A multidisciplinary artist with over 12 years of experience across oil, pencil sketch, and digital mediums. Anshika's work draws from both the classical traditions of Indian painting and the dynamic energy of contemporary digital art.

She has exhibited at galleries in Mumbai, Delhi, and internationally, and her work has been collected by patrons across 20+ countries. Her passion lies in making art accessible, meaningful, and emotionally resonant for everyone.
